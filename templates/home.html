Features

Chat As Doctor

The "Chat as Doctor" feature enables users to practice diagnosing virtual patients by simulating real-world medical scenarios. Users take on the role of a doctor, interact with patients, and analyze symptoms to make informed diagnoses. The AI evaluates the user's diagnostic decisions, providing real-time feedback and scoring accuracy. This feature helps improve diagnostic and treatment skills through interactive practice.

Chat As Patient

The "Chat as Patient" feature enables users to act as patients, describing their symptoms to the AI, which functions as the doctor. The AI diagnoses the condition based on the provided symptoms and delivers a treatment plan, offering medical advice. This interactive feature helps users understand potential diagnoses, receive treatment suggestions, and gain insight into healthcare processes.

Quick Diagnosis

The "Quick Diagnosis" feature enables fast identification of specific diseases like Alzheimer's, heart disease, and diabetes. By analyzing key symptoms and medical history, the AI provides rapid and accurate diagnoses. This feature aims to offer users quick insights into these critical conditions, helping them understand potential health issues and guiding further medical consultation or treatment.

Profile and History

The "Profile and History" feature allows users to maintain a detailed record of their medical background, including past diagnoses, treatments, allergies, and family medical history. This feature helps in providing personalized diagnoses, treatment plans, and better management of health. It allows users to track their symptoms, conditions, and treatments over time for more informed healthcare decisions.
